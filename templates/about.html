About Attendify

Revolutionizing attendance management with cutting-edge QR technology and real-time analytics

Our Story

Attendify was born from a simple idea: attendance tracking should be effortless, accurate, and accessible to everyone. Founded in 2024, we've grown from a classroom project to serving thousands of users worldwide.

Our platform combines the simplicity of QR codes with powerful analytics to give organizations complete control over their attendance data.

Our Mission

To eliminate the headaches of traditional attendance systems by providing a seamless, secure, and user-friendly platform that saves time and improves accuracy.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for attendance management, empowering organizations of all sizes with data-driven insights to optimize their operations.
